The great white

great white sharks arent too fond of eating humans, but if your on a small boat that looks like a seal, it is possible that they can attack.

Things the great whites like to eat

  • small fish
  • big fish
  • seals
  • anything with a pulse that can swim basically

Top things great whites dont eat in order

  1. humans
  2. lions
  3. other great white sharks
  4. cast iron anchors (although they will try)
